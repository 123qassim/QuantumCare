About Corona Virus

COVID-19, caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, emerged in December 2019 and rapidly spread worldwide, leading to a global pandemic. It primarily affects the respiratory system, with symptoms ranging from mild to severe, including fever, cough, shortness of breath, and loss of taste or smell. In some cases, the virus can cause pneumonia, organ failure, or even death, especially in individuals with underlying health conditions

Coronavirus what it is?

Coronavirus (COVID-19) is a highly contagious virus caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It primarily affects the respiratory system, with symptoms like fever, cough, and shortness of breath.
