Oral hygiene prevents plaque build-up, strengthens enamel, and supports overall well-being. Regular brushing, flossing, and professional cleanings are your best defense against oral issues.

Beyond physical health, oral hygiene can also improve your social and professional life. A clean smile and fresh breath make every interaction more enjoyable. Remember to replace your toothbrush regularly and use fluoride toothpaste.
